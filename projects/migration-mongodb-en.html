Data quality and reliability

Implementation: duplicate detection/removal, integrity checks, and strict type conversion

Business value: higher confidence in post-migration analyses

Industrialization and automation

Implementation: reusable scripts, pytest/mongomock tests, and Docker Compose execution

Business value: simpler and reproducible deployment across environments

Access governance and baseline security

Implementation: dbOwner/read-only account creation with write/read permission checks

Business value: reduced risk of non-compliant use of medical data

Business analysis on migrated data

Implementation: MongoDB aggregation routines (hospitals, pathologies, medications, blood groups)

Business value: immediate use of data for decision-making
