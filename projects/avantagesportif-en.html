Medallion data pipeline design

Business value: better pipeline readability and simpler maintenance

HR and business data reliability

Implementation: data normalization, declarative SODA checks, and quality-result tracking

Business value: reduced decision risk from inconsistent data

Business parameterization for steering

Implementation: benefit calculation driven by config tables (rates, thresholds, eligibility rules)

Business value: fast adaptation to HR policy changes without code rewrites

Operational orchestration and automation

Implementation: Kestra flows, Flask API execution, and Slack event notifications

Business value: clear monitoring and fewer manual operations

Decision support delivery

Implementation: structured Tableau exports and cost/eligibility-oriented reporting prep

Business value: supports HR/finance trade-off decisions on the benefits program
