Implemented skills

This section mirrors the competencies described in annexes.md for this project.

Data audit and root-cause diagnosis

Implementation: revenue comparison by log dates, cross-check between OLAP facts and technical logs, validated with SQL

Business value: objective identification of the source of decision-making discrepancies

Consistency verification and quality control

Implementation: separation of valid/invalid facts, fact-dimension consistency checks, and identifier controls

Business value: greater reliability of business indicators used in reporting

Transactional hardening and integrity

Implementation: SQLite triggers (blocking retroactive inserts and inconsistent deletions), FK activation, and Batch_Control table

Business value: reduced risk of historical KPI drift

Data architecture and analytical operations

Implementation: OLTP -> batch -> OLAP -> dataviz flow, target star schema, and SQL audit scripts

Business value: clear technical framework to industrialize data supervision

Professional reporting and action plan

Implementation: structured audit report, prioritized recommendations, and implementation roadmap

Business value: from technical findings to an operational roadmap
