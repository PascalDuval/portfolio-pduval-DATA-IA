Implemented skills

Streaming + batch pipeline design

Implementation: real-time ingestion via Kafka topic and snapshot batch processing with PySpark

Business value: robust combination of continuous capture and consolidated KPI production

Business event enrichment and modeling

Implementation: explicit ticket schema, field standardization, and support-team derivation

Business value: more interpretable data for support routing and operations steering

Multi-format analytical output production

Implementation: Parquet, JSON, and CSV exports for data science, BI, and reporting use cases

Business value: easier interoperability with downstream tools

Containerization and technical integration

Implementation: Docker Compose orchestration of Redpanda, producer, and PySpark job

Business value: reproducible local deployment and reduced environment drift

Technical quality and testability

Implementation: broker connectivity tests, PySpark smoke tests, and containerized end-to-end validation

Business value: quick regression detection and safer deliveries
